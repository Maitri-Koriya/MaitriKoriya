Rhythm Wave – A Modern Web Platform for Music Lovers

Rhythm-Wave is a visually engaging and fully responsive music website crafted to deliver a seamless and immersive music experience. Designed with music enthusiasts in mind, it allows users to explore and play tracks through an intuitive and stylish interface.

Project information

  • Category: Front-End Web Application
  • Client: Academic
  • Project date: 11 December, 2024
  • Project URL: https://rhythm-wave-maitri-koriyas-projects.vercel.app/

Key Features

  • Play/pause and volume control.
  • Users can download songs directly from the platform.
  • Allows users to upload and share their own songs.
  • Built using HTML, CSS, php, JavaScript.
